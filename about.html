About

Why this project exists

Roumieh Quant is a front-end project that brings three things engineering students breaking into quant finance actually need into one place: a pulse on the markets, a vetted reading list, and a CV format the rest of the world can read.

Stack

How it's built

Semantic HTML5, hand-written CSS3 and Bootstrap 5 for layout, and vanilla JavaScript written entirely with ES6 classes — no jQuery, no frameworks.

Data

Where the data comes from

Live quotes come from the Finnhub API. The watchlist and the resource shelf are curated by hand — real companies and real references, not placeholders.
